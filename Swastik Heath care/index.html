About our Doctors

The History of Our Organisation


Swastik Health Care has its roots in a vision to provide exceptional medical services to the community. Founded in the early 2000s, it began as a modest clinic with a mission to offer affordable and quality healthcare. Over the years, Swastik Health Care has grown into a state-of-the-art facility, integrating advanced medical technology and highly skilled professionals. Its unwavering commitment to patient care and wellness has established it as a trusted name in the region, delivering compassionate treatment and pioneering healthcare solutions.


Physiotherapy

"Physiotherapy is a healthcare discipline focused on restoring, maintaining, and enhancing physical function and mobility. It combines manual therapy, exercise, and patient education to alleviate pain, improve strength, and prevent future injuries."

Orthopedic

Orthopedics is a specialized branch of medicine focused on diagnosing, treating, and preventing disorders related to the musculoskeletal system, including bones, joints, muscles, ligaments, and tendons. Orthopedic care is essential for addressing conditions like fractures, arthritis, spine disorders, and sports injuries."

Cardiology

"Cardiology is the branch of medicine dedicated to diagnosing and treating disorders of the heart and blood vessels. It focuses on conditions such as coronary artery disease, heart failure, arrhythmias, and hypertension. "
